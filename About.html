taylor regan

Co-Founder | Brand & Web Designer

My journey began as Digital Strategist at a national PR firm. This is where I honed my skills in creating impactful digital campaigns and developing effective brand strategies. At the same time, this period in my life left me daydreaming about starting my own small business.

Following my career in PR, I chose to pursue that entrepreneurial passion and founded an e-commerce business. My business quickly gained traction became competitive with the top brands in my market -- all without spending a dime on advertising. I learned how to utilize a brand identity and social media to attract my customer base. Through this venture, I also gained valuable experience in web design, e-commerce platforms including Shopify and Etsy, and fine-tuned my skills in illustration and graphic design.

Today, with over 10 years of experience, I know how to create visually stunning brands that are effective across all media channels.

loves: spending time on her family's farm, growing flowers, D&D, tabletop & PC games, her two cats.

dislikes: when people don’t answer phones quickly enough in movies, ticks, rollercoasters, sticky floors.
